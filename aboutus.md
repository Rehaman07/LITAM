# Loyola Institute of Technology and Management (LITAM)

Loyola Institute of Technology and Management (LITAM) is a private engineering and management institution located in the Palnadu/Guntur region of Andhra Pradesh, India. Established in 2001, LITAM operates under the Santhi Nikethan Minority Education Society and serves students from rural, semi-urban, and urban communities.

## Institutional Profile

- Established: 2001
- Affiliation: Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada (JNTUK)
- Approval: All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi
- Certification: ISO 9001:2015 certified institution
- Accreditation: NAAC "A" Grade institution
- Campus: 27-acre green campus with more than 232,102 square feet of built-up area
- Code: AP EAPCET / ECET / PGECET code LOYL; AP ICET / AP POLYCET code LITM

## Facilities and Infrastructure

LITAM provides modern departmental laboratories, digital computer labs, a central library, sports spaces, transport facilities, hostels, a canteen, and student support systems. The campus environment is designed to support classroom learning, practical exposure, career readiness, and student wellbeing.

## Placements and Industry Network

LITAM operates a dedicated training and placement cell that conducts placement preparation throughout the academic year. Recruiters associated with student placement opportunities include TCS, Wipro, Infosys, Capgemini, Tech Mahindra, HCLTech, Accenture, and Cognizant.
